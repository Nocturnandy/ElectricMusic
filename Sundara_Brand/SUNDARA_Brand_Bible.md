# SUNDARA — Brand Bible

*Electric. Every Sunday.*

---

## 1. The Idea

SUNDARA is a weekly Sunday night ritual for underground electronic music — a room that runs from dusk to 3am while the rest of the city winds down. The name comes from the Sanskrit *sundara*, meaning **beautiful / radiant**. The visual world flips that on its head: a sun in total eclipse — its light pulled inward, colour bleeding only at the rim. Beauty in the dark. Light you feel rather than see.

Everything ladders up to one line:

> **Electric. Every Sunday.**

---

## 3. Voice & Tone

Short. Lowercase-cool. Confident, never shouty. SUNDARA states things; it doesn't sell them. Three to five words beats a sentence. Let the silence and the space do the work — the same way the artwork does.

Do: *No sun. Just pulse.*
Don't: *Come down for the biggest party of your week!!!*

---

## 4. Logo

Three separate, layered assets — keep them independent so they can be recomposed across formats.

- **`sundara_wordmark_transparent.png`** — the SUNDARA logotype. Fat, rounded, groovy bubble letterforms in stone grey on a transparent background.
- **`sundara_gradient_bg.png`** — the lavender-to-white gradient field. The default canvas.
- **`sundara_sun_transparent.png`** — the eclipsed sun, transparent PNG. The hero symbol; can stand entirely on its own.
- **`sundara_lockup_preview.png`** — reference composition of all three together.

**Rules**

- Keep clear space around the wordmark equal to the height of the **S** on all sides.
- The sun sits **above** the wordmark in vertical formats; never overlap the two.
- Wordmark stays a single flat colour — stone grey on light, or near-white (#F9F6FB) when placed on the eclipse / dark fields.
- Don't recolour the wordmark into the spectrum, add outlines, drop shadows, or squash the proportions.
- The sun is the only element allowed to carry colour. Everything else stays neutral.

## 6. Typography

- **Display / wordmark** — the groovy rounded bubble logotype (supplied as artwork). Used only for the SUNDARA name.
- **Headlines & line-up** — a clean, slightly condensed mono or grotesque, uppercase, wide tracking. Sits underneath the logo (e.g. `SUN 21 JUNE`, artist names).
- **Utility / details** — same mono for times, address, tags. Small caps, evenly spaced: `■ ELECTRIC` · `EVERY SUNDAY ■` · `8PM–3AM`.

Keep type quiet so the sun does the talking.
