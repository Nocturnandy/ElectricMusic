# SUNDARA — Copy System

*Retuned to the Electric house voice — sunrise / first-light, "Stay For Sunrise."*

**Voice:** Elevated underground. Confident, tasteful, restrained — sits next to *Midnite Arcade* and the rest of the Electric roster. Tight imperative mottos ("Play Past Midnite," "Stay For Sunrise"). No exclamation marks, no hype words ("epic," "insane," "vibes"), no countdown urgency. Short lines. Serious about the music, light on its feet.

**The shift:** SUNDARA isn't the dark of the night — it's the other end of it. The deep house and underground grooves that carry you *through* to first light. The sun in our mark isn't setting. It's about to break.

**Rule of thumb:** if it sounds like it's trying to fill the room, cut it. The room fills itself.

## 10. Manifesto / About

**Short (web header, ~40 words)**

> SUNDARA is a Sunday room for underground and deep house in Prahran — sunrise grooves to carry the night home. From dusk till first light. We treat the end of the week as the best part of it. Stay for the sunrise.

**Long (about page / press, ~120 words)**

> SUNDARA takes its name from the Sanskrit word for *beautiful*. Our mark is a sun caught at the edge of breaking — light gathering, colour bleeding at the rim. Not the end of the night. The turn of it.

> Every Sunday, while the city winds down, we open a low-lit room on Chapel Street and let it run from dusk through to first light. Underground house, deep house, tech house, techno — booked for the heads, not the hype. No bottle-service theatre, no phones in the air. Just a sound system, a careful line-up, and a floor full of people who'd rather take the long way home.

> The weekend doesn't have to end. It can deepen — right through to the sunrise.

> Stay For Sunrise.
