01 ABOUT

SUNDARA is an independent music project & label exploring organic, melodic, sun-drenched electronic music.

SUNRISE NEVER SOUNDED SO GOOD.

Based in Naarm / Melbourne, Australia.

02 BRAND STORY

Sundara — Sanskrit for "beautiful." If Midnite Arcade is the last record of the night, Sundara is the first light of the morning. Music for dawn dancefloors, daylight sessions and golden hours.

Where the night ends, the day begins.

03 LOGO — USED AS IS

The groovy 70s wordmark is the fixed master asset. Never redraw, retype, recolour or distort. Clearspace: height of the "S" on all sides. Source: SUNDARA-MAIN-BRAND.png / EL_Sundara Working File_V1.ai

04 BRAND ART

Bauhaus-style geometric suns — quarter circles, halves and full circles in navy & orange on cream. The system: circles rising, like the sun.
